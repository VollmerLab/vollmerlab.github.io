A Network Approach to White Band Disease Challenged Staghorn Coral Acropora cervicornis microRNAs and Their Targets

Despard, B.A., Selwyn, J.D., Shupp, A.N. and Vollmer, S.V.

Antibiotic pretreatment inhibits white band disease infection by suppressing the bacterial pathobiome

Selwyn, J. D., Despard, B. A., Galvan-Dubois, K. A., Trytten, E. C., & Vollmer, S. V.

Genomic signatures of disease resistance in endangered staghorn corals

Vollmer, S. V., Selwyn, J. D., Despard, B. A., & Roesel, C. L.

Science, 381(6665), 1451-1454 (2023)

Breakthrough genome-wide association study identifying 10 genomic regions and 73 SNPs associated with white band disease resistance. Shows that polygenic scores can accurately predict disease resistance in Caribbean staghorn corals, providing critical tools for coral restoration efforts.
